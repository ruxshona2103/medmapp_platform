Your Path to Recovery

Our care covers every stage of your journey.

1

Free Pre-Travel Services

  • Opinions and price quotes from multiple clinics based on your medical reports.
  • Selection of the best doctor and clinic matching your condition and budget.
  • Arrangement of a video consultation with the chosen doctor before your trip.
  • Preparation of the invitation letter from the clinic and necessary documents for a medical visa.
2

Support During Your Trip

  • Airport pickup with a private car and transfer to the hotel/clinic.
  • Professional medical interpreter services for communication with the doctor at the clinic.
  • Booking accommodation (hotel, apartment) near the clinic according to your budget.
  • 24/7 contact for any questions that may arise during your trip.
3

Post-Travel Care

  • Organization of online follow-ups with the doctor after treatment.
  • Provision of translated copies of all medical summaries and reports.
  • Assistance and advice on further treatment courses or medications.
