About the Clinic

Opened in August 2022 in Faridabad, Amrita Hospital is the largest private hospital in India and part of the globally renowned Mata Amritanandamayi Math network. Since its inauguration, this hospital has been a beacon of medical excellence.

The clinic is committed to providing patients with holistic, world-class medical care. Strategically located in Sector 88, this premier facility is easily accessible from the Delhi NCR region.

Advanced Infrastructure

  • World-Class Medical Campus: Spanning over 130 acres with a built-up area of 1 million square meters.
  • Specialized Centers: A comprehensive Cancer Center and an organ transplant department (kidney, liver, and heart).
  • Latest Technologies: Advanced radiology services equipped with PET CT, 3T MRI, and other modern imaging systems.
  • Robotic Surgery: Minimally invasive robotic systems.
  • Artificial Intelligence Integration: AI and Machine Learning for precise diagnostics and personalized treatment plans.
  • Eco-Friendly Design: Solar power systems and water recycling plants.
